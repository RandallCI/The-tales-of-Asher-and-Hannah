One day, it was such a lovely day, that Ash and Hannah were playing outside. Ash was playing on the see-saw in their garden and Hannah wanted to see how high she could jump. The problem was, the see-saw was not much fun when you were only one person and jumping on her own turned out to be not much fun when doing it alone today.
