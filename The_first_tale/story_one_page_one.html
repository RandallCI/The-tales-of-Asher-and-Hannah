One lovely, sunny day, brother and sister, Ash and Hannah were playing outside. Ash was playing on the see-saw in the garden and Hannah wanted to see how high she could jump. The problem was, the see-saw was not much fun when you were only one person and jumping on her own turned out to be not much fun when doing it alone.
